# Exp 8: Reproducing an Image Using Prompts for Image Generation

## Date : 24/05/2026

## Reg. No. : 212223220088

## Aim:

To demonstrate the ability of text-to-image generation tools to reproduce an existing image by crafting precise prompts. The goal is to identify key elements within the image and use these details to generate an image as close as possible to the original.

---

# Futuristic City at Night

## Basic Prompt:

“A futuristic city with neon lights at night.”

## Refined Prompt:

“A cyberpunk-style futuristic city at night with glowing neon signs, tall skyscrapers, wet roads reflecting colorful lights, flying vehicles, and a dark atmospheric background.”

## Final Detailed Prompt:

“A detailed cyberpunk futuristic cityscape at night featuring massive illuminated skyscrapers, neon blue and pink lights, holographic advertisements, flying cars in the sky, rain-soaked streets reflecting vibrant colors, cinematic shadows, realistic urban textures, high-detail sci-fi environment, ultra realistic digital art style, dramatic perspective, glowing windows, and atmospheric fog.”

## Tool Used:

* DALL·E / Stable Diffusion / MidJourney

## Comparison Report:

The generated image successfully recreated the futuristic atmosphere and neon lighting of the original image. Reflections on wet roads and skyscraper details were visually accurate. Some variations appeared in vehicle placement and advertisement designs. Prompt refinement with additional texture and lighting details improved similarity with the reference image.

## Conclusion:

The experiment demonstrated that carefully crafted prompts significantly improve the quality and accuracy of AI-generated images. By refining prompts with detailed descriptions of colors, lighting, textures, composition, and artistic style, image generation models can reproduce visuals closely resembling the original images. The experiment also highlighted the importance of iterative prompt engineering in achieving high-quality image outputs.
